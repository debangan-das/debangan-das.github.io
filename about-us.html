About Us

We are a team of photographers and cinematographers, from Kolkata, West Bengal,India. We are based out of Kolkata but we serve wherever our work takes us.

Our team of photographers and cinematographers have been capturing beautiful and candid moments in weddings, pre-weddings and cute moments of babies for the past 6 years individually in different photography organizations. Then we joined hands and founded Grafytree. Now we have been working as a team for the past 3 years.

Our main focus is always making our clients comfortable in any situation and capture those beautiful moments on your special day so that you can relive them forever.

We believe in letting our work speak for itself. You can check out our work more extensively in our various social media handles.
